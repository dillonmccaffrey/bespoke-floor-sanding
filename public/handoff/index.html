1. Getting Google Reviews

‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê THIS IS THE #1 MOST IMPORTANT THING FOR SEO ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê

Critical: Reviews are the single biggest factor holding back your Dublin rankings. You're currently #30 for "floor sanding dublin" ‚Äì getting 10+ reviews with location mentions could push you to page 1. Aim for as many reviews as possible. Every single review helps.

Reviews directly impact your Google ranking. The more reviews you have (especially ones mentioning locations), the higher you'll rank.

How to Get Your Review Link

  1. Open Google Maps on your phone
  2. Search for "Bespoke Floor Sanding"
  3. Tap your business listing
  4. Tap "Customers" ‚Üí "Get more reviews"
  5. Copy the link

Tips for Better Reviews

  • Ask EVERY customer ‚Äì don't be shy, most people are happy to help
  • Ask within 2-3 days of completing work (while it's fresh)
  • Ask them to mention the location in their review (Dublin, Monaghan, Cavan, etc.)
  • Aim for at least 5 reviews per month ‚Äì more is better
  • Respond to every review (even just "Thanks!")
  • Goal: 20+ reviews total ‚Äì this is what top competitors have

2. Adding Photos to Google

üì∏ Photos help customers trust you and improve your ranking.

Steps

  1. Go to https://business.google.com on your computer
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Click on "Bespoke Floor Sanding"
  4. Click "Add photo" (or find Photos in the menu)
  5. Upload your before/after photos
