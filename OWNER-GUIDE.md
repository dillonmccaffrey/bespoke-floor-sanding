## How to Update Content

### 1. Access the Content Manager

1. Go to `https://bespokefloorsanding.ie/admin`
2. Log in with your credentials (provided separately)

### 2. Update Testimonials

1. Click **Testimonials** in the left sidebar
2. To add new: Click **New Testimonial**
3. Fill in:
   - **Name**: Customer's first name
   - **Location**: Where they're from (Dublin, Monaghan, etc.)
   - **Quote**: Their review
   - **Rating**: 1-5 stars
   - **Order**: Lower numbers appear first
4. Click **Publish**

### 3. Add Gallery Images (Before/After)

1. Click **Gallery** in the left sidebar
2. Click **New Gallery**
3. Fill in:
   - **Title**: e.g., "Living Room Restoration"
   - **Location**: Where the job was done
   - **Description**: Brief description
   - **Before Image**: Upload the before photo
   - **After Image**: Upload the after photo
4. Click **Publish**

**Image Tips**:
- Use landscape photos (horizontal)
- Keep file sizes under 2MB
- JPG format is best

### 4. Update Services

1. Click **Services** in the left sidebar
2. Click on a service to edit it
3. Update the title, description, or icon
4. Click **Publish**
